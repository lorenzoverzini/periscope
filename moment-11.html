Small entertainment corners to keep guests engaged
  • Small entertainment corners disseminated throughout the whole venue that generate guests' curiosity and keep them engaged during the whole experience (e.g., mini skateboard corner);​
  • Temporary installation in strategic locations to surprise guests and tease them to try new attractions (e.g., close to queues, to engage during waiting times)​
  • Micro-attractions as fixed physical elements linked to the complex main activities, to surprise and enrich the experience (e.g. small walls to climb close to the main climbing activity);​
  • Ever-new entertainment opportunities to keep people interested in discovering new activities from previous visits​
