Engaging and ever-evolving guests digital alter-egos
  • Guest' digital alter ego: the avatar
  • Avatars are created, updated and assigned a role by guests​
  • Raise the hype and immerse guests in the experience prior to the visit to complexes​
  • Connect with other guests and share experiences​
  • Avatar can be 3D printed once the guests are in the complex​
  • Achievements in the real world are collected through the avatar's growth (e.g. badges)​
  • Avatars track the guests' history inside SEVEN complexes​
  • Avatars are connected to social media and can be used as Snapchat filters to share and play with friends​
