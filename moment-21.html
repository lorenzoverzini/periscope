Personalised communications to engage guests around the new stories
  • Send updates about the new stories related to the attraction through guests' preferred channel (e.g. social media, app, email, ...)​
  • Send invitation to guests' to get to know the new story;​
  • Invitation linked to guests' personal interests and previous experiences;​
  • Contextual communication based on different moments of the experience (e.g., inside vs outside the complex)​
  • Communication type is adapted to channel and preferences expressed by guests (e.g., preference for online communication, frequency must be less than 4 emails/month, etc.)​
