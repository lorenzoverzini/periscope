Interactive installations around KSA to inspire and connect people outside and inside the venues
  • Installations to be located across SKA, possibility to then expand also into other countries' main cities;​
  • Interactive installations settled in key points of cities (e.g. airports, shopping malls, etc.);
  • Installations are also included in each one of the SEVEN venues to provide visibility about what's going on inside the complex;
  • Inside the complexes, installations will be located in strategic points to show key events going on (e.g., parades) and in highly interactive areas for the guests;
  • Allow passers-by to discover SEVEN complexes;
  • Connect people outside and inside the venues;
  • Provide real-time interaction between passers-by and SEVEN characters from the different complexes;​
