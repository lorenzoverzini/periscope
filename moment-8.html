Digital world as a space to tease the experience, before and after visit
  • Games and activities in digital world (e.g. mini video-games) for people to "test-drive" and discover the attractions before their visit and continuing playing also afterwards;​
  • Guests' will "unlock" the games based on the attraction they have booked;
  • Before going to a complex, virtual experiences will create anticipation and excitement around the upcoming visit;
  • After the visit, virtual experiences will enable guests to keep earning points to be redeemed at the complex;​
  • Possibility for guests to create and customise their avatars with digital products unlocked during the visit;​
