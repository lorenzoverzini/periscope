Waiting moments as key parts of the experience
  • Waiting time as an active experience for guests to have fun​
  • Storytelling and games to get immersed even before starting​
  • Interactive edutainment as part of the preparation on the activities (live quizzes, briefings, etc.)​
  • Collect "bonus" from the preparation activities that you can spend during the attraction (e.g. extra time for your challenge)​
  • Place in line always guaranteed while you have fun​
  • Activities tailored to guests' age and skill level​
  • Modular activities to be "switched on/off" depending on waiting time​
